The AutHentiCity Tour

This bike tour takes you through the beauty of Douro Riverside. Bring your friends, have fun and drink Beirão!

beirão

Enjoy the Beirão liqueur drink and another cocktails while you are exploring Oporto and playing a quiz that is going to make you meet new people and cultures or straiten the bonds with your friends.
Our bike tours are led by experienced English speaking guides, who aim to show you the best of Oporto highlights and a professional barman that is going to prepare you the most delicious cocktails with the most famous portuguese liqueur, the Beirão.

Our tours lasts at minimum of 30 min and covers the main areas of the city centre, as well as important city landmarks and Sigthsclubs.
